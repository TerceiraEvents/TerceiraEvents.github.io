If you've walked past **Twins** in Angra recently and found the doors closed, you're not imagining it. They're on a temporary closure with no reopening date announced.

The team posted a statement on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/p/DVRbiLSDDg7/) acknowledging the silence and promising a return. They say it's not an ending but a pause, and that when the doors open again, it'll be impossible to stay indifferent.

### The full statement

Original Portuguese:

> AMIGOS…
>
> Houve um silêncio que caiu sobre a nossa pista.
> Luzes apagadas. Portas fechadas.
> Música suspensa no ar.
>
> Mas nenhum dia passou sem que sentíssemos a vossa falta. Faltaram-nos os abraços apertados, os olhares cúmplices, os brindes que começam tímidos e acabam em celebrações inesquecíveis. Faltou-nos a energia que transforma uma noite num momento eterno.
>
> A nossa ausência foi física… mas o sentimento nunca saiu daqui. Porque esta casa não vive das paredes — vive de vocês.
>
> Se sentiram a nossa falta, saibam que nós sentimos a dobro. E se houve silêncio, foi apenas para prepararmos algo que faça o vosso coração bater ainda mais forte.
>
> Pedimos desculpa pela distância. Nunca foi um fim. Foi uma pausa para voltar maior, mais intensa, mais viva.
>
> Estamos a chegar. Muito em breve. E quando as portas abrirem… vai ser impossível ficar indiferente.
>
> **Preparem-se. ✨ Estamos a chegar.**

English translation *(unofficial, by Terceira Events; corrections welcome)*:

> FRIENDS…
>
> A silence fell over our dance floor.
> Lights out. Doors closed.
> Music suspended in the air.
>
> But not a day has passed without us feeling your absence. We've missed the tight hugs, the knowing glances, the toasts that start shyly and end in unforgettable celebrations. We've missed the energy that turns a night into an eternal moment.
>
> Our absence has been physical… but the feeling never left here. Because this house doesn't live off its walls. It lives off you.
>
> If you've felt our absence, know that we've felt it doubled. And if there's been silence, it was only so we could prepare something that'll make your hearts beat even harder.
>
> We're sorry for the distance. It was never an ending. It was a pause, so we could come back bigger, more intense, more alive.
>
> We're arriving. Very soon. And when the doors open… it'll be impossible to stay indifferent.
>
> **Get ready. ✨ We're arriving.**

### What this means for now

- **Don't plan a night around Twins** until they confirm they're back open.
- Their [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/p/DVRbiLSDDg7/) is the place to watch for the actual reopening announcement.
- If you see a flyer or hear they're back, [let us know](https://github.com/TerceiraEvents/TerceiraEvents.github.io/issues) and we'll update this post.
