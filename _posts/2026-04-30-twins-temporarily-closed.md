If you've walked past **Twins** in Angra recently and found the doors closed, you're not imagining it — they're on a temporary closure with no reopening date announced.

The team posted a statement on [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/p/DVRbiLSDDg7/) acknowledging the silence on the dance floor and the closed doors. The tone is more wistful than commercial: they apologise for the distance, say it was never their wish, and promise to come back with the energy of someone returning after an absence.

What they wrote, in short:

> "Estamos a chegar. Muito em breve." — *We're arriving. Very soon.*

No date, no reason given for the closure, and no advance ticketing or reopening event has been announced.

### What this means for now

- **Don't plan a night around Twins** until they confirm they're back open.
- Their Instagram is the place to watch for the actual reopening announcement.
- If you see a flyer or hear they're back, [let us know](https://github.com/TerceiraEvents/TerceiraEvents.github.io/issues) and we'll update this post.
